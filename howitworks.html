EMPOWERING FARMERS WITH TECHNOLOGY

AGROINVEST is focused on providing the necessary tools and technology for farmers and agribusinesses to boost food production with better yields and lower costs.

Structured financing to help smallholder farmers boost their production capacity

We use our tech-powered network of agro commodity aggregation centres to aggregate key fresh produce, direct from farming communities, giving buyers, processors and service providers access to markets across the entire fresh produce value chain

Step 1
Digital Plaform

Agroinvest provides a secure platform for Nigerians interested in agriculture to participate in Agriculture by selecting the kind of farms they want to sponsor.

Step 2
Farm Selection

Once an individual signs up on our platform by creating an account, they can select the farm units they want to sponsor, subject to availability.

Step 3
Sponsor Farm

We use the sponsor's funds to secure the land, engage the farmer, plant the seeds, insure the farmers and farm produce, complete the full farming cycle, and cover the logistics to sell the harvest.

Step 4
Tracking

While this farm process is ongoing, the farm sponsors are able to keep track of the full-cycle by getting updates in text, pictures and videos from their dashboard via the website or the Agroinvest app. Farm sponsors can also request to go on farm visits so they can speak with the farmers and see how the farms are doing.

Step 5
Return after Harvest

At the end of the farm cycle, we pay and then pay the Farm Sponsor their initial sponsorship and a return on their sponsorship.
